# Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)
## SmartInvest Africa Limited

**Effective Date:** February 4, 2026  
**Version:** 2.0

## 1. POLICY OVERVIEW

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") establishes standards for appropriate use of SmartInvest Africa's Platform, services, and resources. Violation of this policy may result in account suspension, termination, or legal action.

### Policy Scope
This AUP applies to all users, including:
- Individual account holders
- Premium subscribers
- Corporate or institutional users
- API consumers
- Third-party integrations

---

## 2. PROHIBITED ACTIVITIES

### 2.1 Illegal Activity
Users must NOT use the Platform to:

- **Violate Laws:** Engage in any activity illegal under Kenyan law, international laws, or your jurisdiction's laws
- **Money Laundering:** Knowingly facilitate money laundering, structuring transactions to avoid reporting, or obscuring source of funds
- **Sanctions Evasion:** Conduct transactions with sanctioned jurisdictions, entities, or individuals
- **Fraud & Theft:** Engage in fraudulent schemes, identity theft, credit card fraud, or payment fraud
- **Tax Evasion:** Deliberately evade taxes or misrepresent transactions for tax purposes
- **Terrorism Financing:** Provide resources to terrorist organizations or financing terrorism-related activities

### 2.2 Abusive Behavior
Users must NOT:

- **Harassment:** Threaten, harass, or abuse other users or SmartInvest staff
- **Discrimination:** Engage in harassment based on protected characteristics (race, gender, religion, disability, etc.)
- **Defamation:** Make false statements damaging another's reputation
- **Stalking:** Monitor, track, or persistently contact others without consent

### 2.3 Security Violations
Users must NOT:

- **Unauthorized Access:** Attempt to access systems or accounts without authorization
- **Hacking:** Exploit vulnerabilities or attempt to breach platform security
- **Malware:** Upload, distribute, or execute malware, viruses, or ransomware
- **DDoS Attacks:** Conduct denial-of-service or distributed attacks
- **Credential Theft:** Steal or share login credentials, API keys, or tokens
- **Social Engineering:** Attempt manipulation to obtain confidential information

### 2.4 Intellectual Property Violations
Users must NOT:

- **Copyright Infringement:** Reproduce or distribute copyrighted content without permission
- **Trademark Misuse:** Use SmartInvest trademarks without authorization
- **Patent Infringement:** Infringe on patent rights through platform use
- **Content Theft:** Plagiarize or claim others' work as your own
- **Reverse Engineering:** Attempt to reverse engineer proprietary systems

### 2.5 Spam & Abuse
Users must NOT:

- **Unsolicited Messages:** Send spam, phishing, or bulk unsolicited communications
- **Fake Reviews:** Post fraudulent or incentivized reviews or testimonials
- **Manipulation:** Create fake accounts to artificially inflate metrics, votes, or rankings
- **Scraping:** Harvest user data through automated scraping or crawling
- **Referral Abuse:** Create fake referrals or exploit referral programs

### 2.6 Misrepresentation
Users must NOT:

- **False Identity:** Impersonate other individuals, organizations, or entities
- **False Information:** Provide false, misleading, or outdated account information
- **Investment Claims:** Make false claims about investment performance or guarantees
- **Professional Claims:** Falsely claim to be a licensed financial advisor or professional

### 2.7 Financial Crimes
Users must NOT:

- **Ponzi/Pyramid Schemes:** Promote, operate, or participate in such schemes
- **Market Manipulation:** Engage in pump-and-dump schemes or market manipulation
- **Embezzlement:** Misappropriate funds or property
- **Insider Trading:** Trade on material non-public information
- **Chargeback Fraud:** Initiate fraudulent payment disputes or chargebacks

### 2.8 Service Abuse
Users must NOT:

- **Resource Exhaustion:** Consume excessive bandwidth or computing resources
- **API Abuse:** Exceed rate limits, make unauthorized API calls, or abuse API terms
- **Account Sharing:** Share premium accounts or credentials with unauthorized parties
- **Automation Violation:** Use bots, scrapers, or automation without written permission
- **Competitive Scraping:** Extract data for competitive purposes

---

## 3. SPECIFIC PROHIBITIONS FOR FINANCIAL TRANSACTIONS

### 3.1 C2B (Customer to Business) Transfers
- Must not be used to circumvent business licensing requirements
- Cannot substitute for regulated financial services
- Must comply with payment processor terms

### 3.2 P2P (Peer to Peer) Transfers
- Cannot facilitate illegal transfers between parties
- Must not be used for loan sharking, predatory lending, or loan laundering
- Cannot facilitate gambling or betting without proper licenses
- Must not circumvent cross-border payment regulations

### 3.3 Casual Transfers
- Must be for legitimate, legal purposes
- Cannot be used to conceal the nature or source of funds
- Must comply with beneficial ownership disclosure requirements
- Cannot facilitate sanctions evasion

### 3.4 Investment-Related Transactions
- Cannot be used to solicit unregistered securities
- Must not facilitate unlicensed investment advice
- Cannot promote high-yield investment programs (HYIP) or scams
- Must comply with capital market regulations

## 4. COMPLIANCE WITH REGULATIONS

### 4.1 Know Your Customer (KYC)
Users must:
- Provide accurate, verifiable identity information
- Maintain current account information
- Verify beneficial ownership when applicable
- Cooperate with identity verification requests

### 4.2 Anti-Money Laundering (AML)
Users must NOT:
- Structure transactions to avoid reporting thresholds
- Use multiple accounts to bypass transaction limits
- Conduct transactions using source of funds known to be illegal
- Facilitate money laundering for others

### 4.3 Regulatory Cooperation
Users must:
- Cooperate with regulatory investigations
- Provide requested documentation to SmartInvest
- Comply with government information requests
- Not interfere with compliance monitoring

### 4.4 Geographic Compliance
Users acknowledge:
- Different jurisdictions have different regulations
- SmartInvest complies with applicable regulations in user's jurisdiction
- Some services may be restricted in certain jurisdictions
- User responsibility to verify local compliance

## 5. CONSEQUENCES OF VIOLATION

### 5.1 Progressive Enforcement

**First Violation:**
- Warning notification
- Account review and possible restrictions
- Educational resources provided
- Opportunity to cure violations (7-30 days depending on severity)

**Repeated Violations:**
- Account restrictions or temporary suspension
- Escalation review by compliance team
- Potential permanent termination

**Severe Violations:**
- Immediate account termination
- Potential legal action
- Referral to law enforcement or relevant authorities
- Preservation of records for legal proceedings

### 5.2 Account Suspension & Termination
Upon violation, we may immediately:
- Restrict account access
- Freeze balances or transactions
- Preserve data for legal proceedings
- Report to relevant authorities as required by law
- Decline future account applications

### 5.3 Financial Consequences
- Forfeiture of premium fees (no refunds)
- Retained balance available per regulation
- Potential liability for damages
- Legal fees and costs

### 5.4 Legal Action
We reserve the right to:
- Pursue civil litigation
- Seek injunctive relief
- Report to law enforcement
- Participate in regulatory investigations
- Enforce indemnification provisions

## 6. MONITORING & ENFORCEMENT

### 6.1 Monitoring Methods
SmartInvest may:
- Monitor transactions for suspicious patterns
- Use automated detection systems
- Review user activities and communications
- Conduct periodic compliance audits
- Cooperate with law enforcement

### 6.2 Detection Systems
- **Behavioral Analysis:** Identify unusual account activity
- **Pattern Recognition:** Detect structuring or manipulation
- **KYC/AML Screening:** Cross-reference against sanction lists and databases
- **Rate Monitoring:** Track transaction frequency and amounts
- **Geolocation Analysis:** Identify suspicious geographic patterns

### 6.3 Third-Party Reporting
We may report violations to:
- Central Bank of Kenya (CBK)
- Financial Reporting Centre (FRC)
- Law enforcement agencies
- Regulatory authorities
- Payment processor networks
- Relevant international bodies

## 7. REPORTING VIOLATIONS

### 7.1 User Reporting
If you observe violations, report to:
- **Email:** abuse@smartinvest.africa
- **Online Form:** Visit https://smartinvest.africa/report-violation
- **Emergency (Fraud/Security):** security@smartinvest.africa

### 7.2 Report Contents
Include:
- Specific violation description
- User(s) or accounts involved
- Evidence or transaction details
- Your contact information
- Date and time of incident

### 7.3 Investigation Process
We will:
- Acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
- Conduct confidential investigation
- Take appropriate action if substantiated
- Follow up on resolution
- Protect reporter confidentiality where possible

---

## 8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

### 8.1 Appeals Process
If your account is suspended or terminated, you may appeal by:
- Contacting: appeals@smartinvest.africa
- Providing detailed explanation
- Submitting evidence of compliance
- Response provided within 15 business days

### 8.2 Appeal Considerations
Appeals will consider:
- Context and circumstances
- First-time vs. repeated violations
- Good faith efforts to comply
- Remedial actions taken
- Overall user history

### 8.3 Final Decision
SmartInvest's decision is final except where required by law to provide additional appeals or regulatory review processes.
