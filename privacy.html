1. Introduction

SmartInvest Africa ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our investment platform and services.

We comply with:

  • The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) - EU Regulation 2016/679
  • Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) - South Africa Act 4 of 2013
  • Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019
  • Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) 2019
  • Financial Services Board (FSB) regulations

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Identity Data: Name, date of birth, national ID/passport number, photographs
  • Contact Data: Email address, phone number, physical address
  • Financial Data: Bank account details, payment information, investment history, portfolio data
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information, usage data
  • KYC/AML Data: Proof of identity, proof of residence, source of funds documentation

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use our platform:

  • Log data (access times, pages viewed, IP address)
  • Device information (operating system, browser version)
  • Cookies and tracking technologies
  • Usage analytics and performance metrics

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Service Provision: To create and manage your account, process transactions, and provide investment services
  • Compliance: To comply with legal obligations including KYC, AML, and financial regulations
  • Security: To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and other security threats
  • Communication: To send you service updates, security alerts, and customer support
  • Analytics: To improve our platform, analyze usage patterns, and enhance user experience
  • Marketing: To send promotional materials (with your consent, which can be withdrawn)

Legal Basis (GDPR/POPIA):

  • Contractual necessity for service provision
  • Legal compliance for regulatory requirements
  • Legitimate interests for fraud prevention and security
  • Consent for marketing communications

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data with:

4.1 Service Providers

  • Supabase: Serverless PostgreSQL database and authentication services
  • Payment Processors: M-Pesa, Paystack, Flutterwave, Stripe, PayPal
  • Identity Verification: KYC/AML compliance service providers
  • Email Services: Transactional and marketing email providers

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, including:

  • Regulatory authorities (FSB, Central Banks, Tax Authorities)
  • Law enforcement agencies
  • Court orders and legal processes
  • Protection of our legal rights

4.3 Business Partners

With your consent, we may share data with verified financial institutions for investment opportunities.

5. Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: TLS 1.3 for data in transit, AES-256 for data at rest
  • Infrastructure: Hosted on AWS with automated backups and disaster recovery
  • Database: Supabase PostgreSQL with encryption, Row Level Security, and automated backups
  • Access Controls: Role-based access, multi-factor authentication, audit logs
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments, penetration testing, compliance reviews
  • Employee Training: Data protection and security awareness programs

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR, POPIA, and other applicable laws, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal obligations)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for marketing at any time

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@smartinvest.africa

7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Analytics to improve user experience
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Personalized advertising (with your consent)

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and stored in AWS data centers globally. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • AWS compliance with international data protection standards
  • Regular assessments of data transfer mechanisms

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and maintain your account
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations (typically 7 years for financial records)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

After the retention period, we securely delete or anonymize your data.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated via email or platform notifications.
