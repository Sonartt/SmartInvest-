General Questions

SmartInvest Africa is a comprehensive investment platform designed to democratize investment knowledge, tools, and opportunities across Africa. We provide advanced analytics, compliance management, partnership networks, and multi-payment support to help African investors make informed decisions.

We currently operate in 15+ African countries, including Kenya, South Africa, Nigeria, Ghana, Uganda, Tanzania, Rwanda, and more. Our platform is expanding to additional markets regularly.

Account & Setup

Click the "Dashboard" or "Get Started" button on our homepage. You'll need to provide your email, create a password, and complete KYC (Know Your Customer) verification with your ID and proof of residence.

You'll need: (1) A valid government-issued ID (passport, national ID, or driver's license), (2) Proof of residence (utility bill, bank statement dated within last 3 months), and (3) A selfie photo for identity verification.

Yes! Free accounts can create 1 portfolio, Premium accounts get 5 portfolios, and Enterprise accounts have unlimited portfolios. This allows you to manage different investment strategies separately.

Payments & Pricing

Security & Privacy

We use bank-grade security including TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit, AES-256 encryption for data at rest, hosted on Supabase serverless infrastructure with PostgreSQL Row Level Security. All systems undergo regular security audits and penetration testing.

Yes! We strongly recommend enabling 2FA on your account for additional security. You can set it up in your account settings using authenticator apps like Google Authenticator or Authy.

When you close your account, we retain your data for the legally required period (typically 7 years for financial records) to comply with regulatory requirements. After this period, your data is securely deleted or anonymized.

Compliance & Regulations

Yes! We are FSB licensed and comply with POPIA (South Africa), GDPR (EU), Kenya Data Protection Act, NDPR (Nigeria), and other applicable financial regulations across our operating jurisdictions.

We implement comprehensive AML/KYC procedures including identity verification, source of funds checks, ongoing transaction monitoring, and reporting suspicious activities to relevant authorities as required by law.

SmartInvest Africa is a technology platform providing access to investment information and tools. Your actual investments are with regulated financial institutions. We recommend reviewing the protections offered by each institution before investing.
