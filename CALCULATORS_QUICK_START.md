## Understanding Your Results

### Life Insurance Premium
- **What it means**: Monthly cost to maintain that coverage
- **Factors affecting it**: Age, health, smoker status, coverage amount
- **Compare with**: Actual quotes from insurance companies

### Annuity Income
- **What it means**: Monthly income guaranteed for life
- **Break-even**: Age at which total payments exceed investment
- **Compare with**: Fixed vs. variable, immediate vs. deferred

### Retirement Balance
- **What it means**: Projected 401k balance at retirement age
- **Safe withdrawal**: 4% rule = sustainable annual income
- **Compare with**: Your retirement expenses

### Pension Benefit
- **What it means**: Monthly income for life after retirement
- **Options**: Survivor choices reduce benefit slightly
- **Compare with**: Your retirement needs

### Estate Tax
- **What it means**: Federal tax owed if estate exceeds exemption
- **2024 Exemption**: $13.61 million per person
- **Planning**: Life insurance, trusts, gifting strategies
