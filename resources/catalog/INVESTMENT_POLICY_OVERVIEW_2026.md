# Investment Policy Overview 2026

## Purpose
This policy summarizes SmartInvest portfolio governance for 2026, focusing on capital preservation, risk-adjusted growth, and transparent reporting.

## Risk posture
- Target volatility: 8–12% annualized
- Maximum drawdown tolerance: 18%
- Liquidity buffer: minimum 10% cash or cash equivalents

## Asset allocation bands
| Asset class | Strategic band | Tactical range |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Equities | 45% | 35–55% |
| Fixed Income | 30% | 20–40% |
| Alternatives | 15% | 10–20% |
| Cash | 10% | 5–15% |

## Governance and reviews
- Allocation review cadence: monthly
- Risk review cadence: bi-weekly
- Policy exception escalation: within 24 hours
