Frequently Asked Questions

What is SmartInvest Africa?

SmartInvest Africa is a financial literacy platform that democratizes investment knowledge, tools, and opportunities across Africa. We provide courses, tools, and community support to help you make informed investment decisions.

How do I get started?

Simply create a free account to access our basic resources. For full access to all courses, tools, and community features, upgrade to Premium with M-Pesa, PayPal, or bank transfer.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa for Kenya and neighboring countries, PayPal for international payments, and bank transfers. All payment methods provide instant access to Premium features.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take security seriously. Your password is encrypted using industry-standard bcrypt hashing. We comply with GDPR and local data protection laws, and we never sell your information.

What does Premium access include?

Premium members get unlimited access to all investment courses, advanced portfolio tracking tools, AI-powered recommendations, weekly webinars, VIP community forum access, and direct mentorship from investment professionals.

Can I cancel my Premium subscription?

Yes, you can contact our support team at any time. Premium access is typically provided for 30 days per payment, and you'll retain access until the period expires.

Do you provide investment advice?

No, SmartInvest Africa provides educational content only. We do not provide personalized financial or investment advice. All investment decisions are your own responsibility, and investments carry risk.
