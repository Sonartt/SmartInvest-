Our Story

Founded in 2024, SmartInvest Africa was born from a simple observation: millions of Africans wanted to invest but lacked access to reliable information, compliant platforms, and trusted partnerships.

Our founders, experienced in both finance and technology across Kenya, South Africa, Nigeria, and Ghana, came together to build a platform that combines regulatory compliance, advanced analytics, and user-friendly design.

Today, we serve over 10,000 users across 15+ African countries, managing $50M+ in assets, and partnering with leading financial institutions to provide verified investment opportunities.

Powered by Supabase serverless PostgreSQL, we ensure world-class security, auto-scaling, and 99.9% uptime for our users.
