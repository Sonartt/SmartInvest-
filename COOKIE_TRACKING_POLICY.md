## 1. COOKIE POLICY OVERVIEW

This Cookie & Data Tracking Policy explains how SmartInvest uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and process information.

### 1.1 What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device that contain information about your browsing activity. SmartInvest uses cookies for:
- Session management
- User preferences
- Security and authentication
- Analytics and performance monitoring
- Personalization and recommendations
- Advertising effectiveness measurement

### 1.2 Policy Scope
This policy covers:
- Cookies stored on user devices
- Web beacons and tracking pixels
- Local storage and session storage
- Mobile app analytics
- Third-party tracking technologies
- All SmartInvest properties and services

### 1.3 Consent Model
By using SmartInvest:
- **Essential Cookies:** Applied automatically (no consent required)
- **Performance/Analytics:** Requires opt-in consent
- **Marketing/Advertising:** Requires explicit opt-in consent
- **Withdrawal:** Users can disable cookies anytime (except essential)

## 3. TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

### 3.1 Web Beacons & Pixels
Tiny invisible images embedded in pages/emails that track:
- Page views and clicks
- Email opens and link clicks
- Conversion events
- User journey mapping

**Use:** Analytics, email marketing, advertising effectiveness  
**Opt-out:** Cookie preferences center

### 3.2 Local Storage & Session Storage
Browser-based storage similar to cookies but with larger capacity.

| Storage Type | Use Case | Data Examples | Duration |
|-------------|----------|----------------|----|
| **Local Storage** | Persistent data | Preferences, saved items | Until cleared |
| **Session Storage** | Temporary data | Form progress, session state | Until browser closed |

### 3.3 Mobile App Analytics
For SmartInvest mobile app:
- Firebase Analytics (Google)
- Crash reporting
- User behavior tracking
- Performance monitoring

**Permissions Required:**
- Device ID (unique identifier)
- Location (if enabled)
- App usage data
- Crash logs

**Opt-out:** Settings > Privacy > Disable analytics

### 3.4 Server-side Tracking
Data collected directly from server logs:
- IP addresses
- User agent information
- Page requests
- Download logs
- Error logs

**Retention:** 90 days (except legal holds)  
**Use:** Security, debugging, compliance

## 5. COOKIE MANAGEMENT & PREFERENCES

### 5.1 Browser-Level Cookie Control

**Chrome:**
1. Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
2. Block all or manage exceptions
3. Search "smartinvest.africa" to remove specific cookies

**Firefox:**
1. Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
2. Remove All or Manage Exceptions
3. See all cookies > Search SmartInvest

**Safari:**
1. Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
2. Search "smartinvest.africa"
3. Click "Remove" or "Remove All"

**Edge:**
1. Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data
2. Select "Cookies and other site data"
3. Click "Clear now"

### 5.2 Cookie Preference Center
On SmartInvest Platform:
1. Log in to your account
2. Settings > Privacy > Cookie Preferences
3. Toggle cookie categories ON/OFF
4. Select "Save Preferences"

**Note:** Disabling essential cookies will limit functionality

### 5.3 Do Not Track (DNT)
If your browser's DNT signal is enabled:
- We respect DNT requests where applicable
- Third-party trackers may not respect DNT
- Some features may not work properly
- Email us at privacy@smartinvest.africa to opt-out manually

### 5.4 Cookie Consent Withdrawal
Change your mind? Withdraw consent anytime:
1. Visit Cookie Preference Center
2. Uncheck desired categories
3. Click "Save Preferences"
4. Existing cookies may not be immediately deleted
5. Email privacy@smartinvest.africa for manual removal

### 5.5 Opt-out of Third-Party Trackers

| Service | Opt-out Link |
|---------|-------------|
| **Google Analytics** | [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) |
| **Facebook** | facebook.com/settings > Ads |
| **Google Ads** | [https://adssettings.google.com](https://adssettings.google.com) |
| **DoubleClick** | [https://www.google.com/ads/preferences](https://www.google.com/ads/preferences) |
